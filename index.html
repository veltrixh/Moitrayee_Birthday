For the overwhelming days

A small reminder to keep close.

Moitrayee, mujhe pata hai exam time pe kabhi kabhi sab kuch ek saath bahut zyada lagne lagta hai. Mind panic karta hai, stress badh jaata hai, aur khud par doubt bhi hota hai. But please remember: ek difficult day tumhari capability decide nahi karta. Breathe. Paani piyo. Thoda rest lo. One chapter, one question, one tiny step at a time—you’ll get through it.

And I’m sorry un moments ke liye jab main tumhare saath utna nahi reh paaya jitna mujhe rehna chahiye tha. It was never because you mattered any less. I may not always have the perfect words or be there at the perfect time, but I’ll always be rooting for you—quietly, genuinely, and with all my heart.

Aur ek baat dil se—I’m really sorry ki kuch din pehle main tum par gussa ho gaya. Mujhe apni feelings ko better way mein express karna chahiye tha; tum us anger ya hurt ki deserving bilkul nahi thi. Main koi excuse nahi dena chahta—bas itna kehna hai ki I genuinely regret it. I never want my anger to make you question how much you matter to me. I’ll try to listen with more patience, speak with more care, and handle things better.

You don’t have to carry everything alone. You are doing better than your anxious mind lets you believe, and I’m very, very proud of you.

Always in your corner, someone very lucky to know you ♡
